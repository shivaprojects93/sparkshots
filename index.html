Behind Every Great Event Is a Photo Worth Keeping!

At SparkShots, we’re all about turning moments into memories. What started as a passion for capturing fun, candid smiles has grown into a full-service PhotoBooth experience designed to elevate any event — from weddings and birthdays to corporate functions and everything in between.

We believe photos should be more than just pictures — they should be an experience. That’s why our booths are packed with creative features like 360 videos, GIFs, instant sharing, custom templates, and stylish props. Every setup is tailored to match your event’s vibe and leave your guests saying, “That was the best part of the night!”

We’re a team of memory-makers who bring energy, professionalism, and a whole lot of fun to every event. Whether you're planning something intimate or extravagant, we’re here to help you capture it — one snap at a time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you serve?

We provide PhotoBooth services for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, holiday parties, school functions, and more!

How long can we rent the PhotoBooth for?

Rental times typically range from 2 to 6 hours, with options for extended hours available upon request.

What do we get with a PhotoBooth rental?

Unlimited photo sessions, instant prints, digital copies, fun props, and a professional attendant to assist during your event.

Can the photo templates be customized?

Absolutely! We personalize templates with your event’s theme, names, dates, logos, or branding.

How soon will we receive the digital photos?

Guests receive their digital photos instantly via email, text, or QR code during the event, and we also provide an online gallery after the event.

Can’t find the answer to your question?

No worries! We’re always happy to help and provide any additional information you need. Feel free to reach out to us anytime—whether it’s a special request or something you want to know, we’re just a message or call away. Your perfect PhotoBooth experience starts with great communication!
